Mackbook Apple

A MacBook is a line of laptops made by Apple, running the macOS operating system and featuring powerful Apple-designed M-series chips. Key features across the lineup include a high-resolution Retina or Liquid Retina display, a trackpad, a Magic Keyboard with Touch ID, and a battery that can last for many hours. Popular models include the MacBook Air, known for its fanless design, and the more powerful MacBook Pro, which has active cooling and a wider array of ports.
